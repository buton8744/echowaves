To get started follow simple steps:

1. create your wave


2. download an iPhone app

     
      https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/echowaves/id782010386

3. start waving on echowaves iPhone app



4. start taking pictures with your iPhone camera



5. switch back to echowaves app on your iPhone (doble-tap home button)



6. the app will immidiately start pushing pictures taken while waving was turned on



7. see your pictures on echowaves.com web site


8. blend your wave with other waves -- this is how you share


Echowaves facts:

Echowaves is not your typical social network:

While there are plenty of social networks in existance, we felt that non of them does exctly what we want -- so the echowaves was born.

Actually, all of social networks these days try to do everything and, as a result, they do not do a specific thing very well.

We strive to keep echowaves simple, secure and reliable.

Echowaves is Micro Photo Blogging:

Take pictures with your phone, as many as you want, where ever you go, what ever you do -- just keep snapping. We strongly believe -- it's much easier to snap a picture, than to type a 140 characters to tell your story.

Echowaves respects your privacy:

We strongly believe that your privacy is what you should be in control, not your social network. As such, it's crucial to keep things simple from the beginning.

In echowaves nothing happens by default -- if you are sending a blending request to other wave -- it must always we confirmed. Only when both waves agree to blend -- only then the blending relationship will be established.

Every modern social network sends out tons of email (spam) -- except for echowaves. Echowaves does not even asks you for your email address when you register a wave -- because we strongly believe, that knowing your email address reveils too much personal information about you, so we avoid it.

Echowaves metaphor:

It helps to think about echowaves as a metaphor. This is very simple, yet -- very strong metaphor. Here is how the metaphor corresponds to the terms you maybe more familier with:

Account == Wave

Sign in to your account == Tune into wave

Sign out == Tune Out

Stream of messages == EchoWave

Sharing == Blending

Waving == telling your story with pictures taken on your iPhone

Echowaves is instant -- there is no quicker way to share a photo stream, than to snap a bunch of pictures on your iPhone, then switch to echowaves application to have them natuarally flow over to your wave.

Over time echowave fades out (just like a real wave, or like an echo) -- you can only see 100 last pictures in your echowave.

Echowaves is still very young:

Echowaves is running in test drive mode -- early adopter's feedback is highly appreciated.

Send your thougts to dmitry@echowaves.com

Echowaves has future:

Our vision is that echowaves will eventually have only mobile phone user inteface -- bear with us...
